## Step 1 — Export the presentation PDF

1. Open `presentation.html` in Chrome or Firefox
2. Press Ctrl+P (Cmd+P on Mac)
3. Destination → **Save as PDF**
4. Layout → Portrait, Margins → None or Minimum
5. Save and rename to `presentation.pdf`
6. Upload to the `📁 Remote Job Auto-Apply Pipeline/` root in Google Drive
